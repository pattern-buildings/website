Pattern Building can be divided into following components:

* room module
* body made up from the modules
* skeleton
* skin
* base
* circulation
* utility systems

  * Ventilation
  * Heating and cooling
  * Water and sewerage
  * Electricity
* Production and transport
* Assembly and construction
* Operation of the Building

  * Energy efficiency
  * Fire safety
  * Acoustics
  * Life Cycle
